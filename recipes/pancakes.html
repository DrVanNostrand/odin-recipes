Pancake Recipe

Description

If you want a delicious pancake recipe, look no further! This recipe will take you about 20 minutes total to finish, and at the end your tastebuds will be thanking you! This recipe makes approximately 8 servings.

I dreamt up this pancake recipe while camping in the majestic Great Smoky Mountains. While camping one night, a bear crept into our campground and stole all our cooking ingredients. I was distraught, but somehow kept my composure and was able to create the world's greatest pancakes using only the foraged forest ingredients around me. Incidentally, I didn't write that recipe down. However, THIS recipe, using common ingredients, is the closest I've come to replicating them.

Ingredients

  • 1 & 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 & 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt, or more to taste
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 & 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons butter, metlted

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg and melted butter; mix until smooth.
  2. Head a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides and serve hot.
